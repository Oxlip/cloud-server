FAQs

What is Nuton?

Nuton is a family of modern and equally advanced set of home appliances to make your daily life easy, safe and fashionable.

How many members are there in Nuton Family?

Nuton family currently has 3 members. Aura, the smart plug, Lyra, the smart touch switch, and an easy to use smart phone application. New members are on its way.

What does Nuton do for me ?

Nuton family members are designed to make you happy. They save your money, electricity and time in the most trendy and convenient ways. Forgot to turn off your electric geyser, Aura can warn you. Want the TV to start recording your favorite show on a certain time, Aura can do it. Not just that, you can control the brightness of your bulbs to match your mood using the extremely easy-to-use your cell phone application. Something you can flaunt !

How does Nuton save electricity ?

Nuton saves electricity by carefully monitoring your electric usage and turning off the devices when not in use. Nuton also detects an empty house and makes sure no electricity is wasted.

Does Nuton need internet to operate ?

Nuton devices are not dependent on Internet. Nuton uses Bluetooth technology and it can be used along with any modern day cell phone.

Are Nuton products safe for my family ?

Nuton is extremely friendly and 100% safe for everyone. Nuton members are designed keeping in mind every safety aspect and its also certified by xxxx"

How does Nuton keep a track of my electricity usage ?

Nuton devices have an inbuilt advanced microchip which keeps track of every unit of energy consumed by your electric appliances. It doesn't matter if the device is under-use or is on stand-by.

Can I just buy Aura or Lyra or do I have to buy both ?

You can simply buy Aura and use it with your smart phone application. For Lyra, you have to also have Aura as Lyra works along with Aura. Lyra can also be controlled by your smart phone.

How do I install Nuton products ?

Nuton members are designed to be a painless experience and it's modern design makes it easier for anybody to simply start using it. No electrician or help required.
