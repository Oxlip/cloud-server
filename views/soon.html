FEATURES

Our Mission is simple - Smart Home For Every House. At Oxlip, we only believe in the best and that is what we are set to deliver. Being an Indian in its origin, Oxlip completely understands the Indian household requirements and has manufactured the products accordingly.

Convenient

Want to have a light switch near sofa or bed? With Oxlip you can do it in a minute. Since our switches are wireless you can just move it and stick it anywhere, anytime and control any appliance(light, fan, TV, fridge etc).

Easy

Our products are extremely easy to install and use. No electricians required to install our products. All our products are ready to use right outside the box and there is no learning curve.

Monitor

Like to monitor your house while you are on vacation? Our intelligent camera can automatically detect people, take photo and send email/alert to your phone.

Affordable

Oxlip suited for every pocket without compromising on the quality. Our wireless switch starts from just Rs.3999. We are able achieve this low cost by mass manufacturing.

Save

Right from day one, Oxlip starts saving you electricity and money. Studies shows smart home saves at least 15% of home electricity bill. Also Oxlip helps you to be environment friendly by reducing energy waste.
